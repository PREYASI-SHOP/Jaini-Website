Sports Psychology


An individualised Sports Psychology training program to improve your performance and enhance your mental well-being. Join the programme today and build your mental strength to overcome pressure, face challenges, build confidence and enhance motivation to excel in your sport and other spheres of life.






For Athletes

1. One-to-One consultations

‘One size doesn’t fit all’ – A one-to-one consultation caters to the individual needs of an athlete to improve performance and mental-wellbeing.

Focus in on understanding the athlete, their goals, expectation, needs, demands of the sport, etc. to tailor an individualised intervention plan that supports an athlete to achieve their goals.


This program will focus on areas such as :




Duration: Approx. 60 mins per session. Online & in-person consultation available.

**This program can be customised as per your needs and requirement





2. Group sessions/workshops

Focus will be on understanding the team expectations, goals, team culture, requirements and areas of improvement to design a plan for the whole team.

Various methods like group workshops, individual consultations with the athletes, coaches or sports-science professionals and game observations will be adopted.


This program will focus on areas such as :



Online & in-person consultation available.

**This program can be customised as per your needs and requirement



For Coaches



This programme is specifically designed for team and individual player coaches. One-to-One consultations & Coach development workshop are conducted to introduce psychological skills and mental exercises to in training and competitions with athletes and teams.


This program will focus on areas such as :


Online & in-person consultation available.

**This program can be customised as per your needs and requirement



For Parents



This programme is specifically designed for parents of the concerned athletes. One-to One consultations and group workshops are conducted to focus on identifying ways in which parents can help in supporting the athletes.
